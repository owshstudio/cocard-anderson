What's covered

Four pillars of cyber liability

Modern cyber coverage is built from four components. We help you understand which ones your business needs.

Errors & Omissions

A third-party form covering negligence or errors in your product or in the performance of your services, including an indirect breach of customer data.

Network Security

First- and third-party coverage for unauthorized access, transmission of a virus or malicious code, theft or destruction of data, cyber extortion, and business interruption.

Privacy

First- and third-party coverage for data exposed by a hacker, a lost device, a rogue employee, or failure to properly destroy physical records.

Media Liability

A third-party form covering infringement of intellectual property and advertising or personal injury arising from your business activities.

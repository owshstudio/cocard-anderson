Why a terminal

When a simple terminal beats a full POS

If you mostly need to take a card and print a receipt, a dedicated terminal is faster to learn, cheaper to run, and rock-solid day to day. We set it up, load it with the right settings, and support it locally.

  • Chip, swipe, and contactless (tap) on every model
  • Countertop or wireless to match how you serve
  • Pairs with Cash Discount (dual pricing) to cut your fees
  • Local setup, training, and a real person on the phone

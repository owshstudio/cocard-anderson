Who we are

Personal service, from people who pick up the phone

Since 2002, we've learned that our customers want things honest and simple. They deserve a clear understanding of their transaction costs, easily accessed resources, and fast answers. In short, we work hard to provide what our customers deserve: value.

Our Syracuse, NY and Bennington, VT operations specialize in personal service and support. As point-of-sale and integrated merchant services experts, we know you have choices. So when you call CoCard Anderson, an employee answers the phone. You'll speak to a professional who becomes a resource you rely on when you need help.

No question is too simple, and no problem too complicated. In fact, we welcome the opportunity to be of service.

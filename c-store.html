What it is

An AI register that does the work you don't want to

Bodega AI is not your standard digital register. It uses AI in real time to track your daily sales and your inventory, and it tells you when a product is running low and needs to be reordered. Loading your catalog is painless: send your most recent inventory as a spreadsheet and Bodega uploads it for you.

It ships with a universal scanner that looks up or adds any item, and even suggests an average sale price based on your zip code. There's a built-in dual pricing integration, a 24/7 AI helper that walks you through any task step by step, and an app that lets you change anything you could change at the register, from anywhere.

  • Real-time AI sales and inventory tracking with low-stock alerts
  • Universal scanner with zip-code-based price recommendations
  • Built-in dual pricing to pass card fees to the customer
  • 24/7 AI helper with step-by-step guidance
  • Companion app, manage your store from anywhere
  • Catalog upload done for you from a simple spreadsheet

The hidden cost

A chargeback costs far more than the sale

When a customer disputes a charge, you don't just refund the amount. You lose the product or service you already delivered, you pay a chargeback fee, and if disputes pile up you risk higher processing rates or losing your merchant account altogether.

Most disputes are winnable with the right evidence, but gathering receipts, signatures, and delivery records for every case is a job no busy owner has time for. That's where we come in.

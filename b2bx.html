Common questions

B2BX FAQ

What is Level 3 data, exactly?

Level 3 data is enhanced information passed with a credit or purchasing card transaction. A simple transaction passes the amount, card number, and expiration. Level 3 adds detail like item number, item description, unit of measure, and invoice number. Businesses and government agencies need this to simplify record-keeping, and Visa and Mastercard give you lower processing rates whenever you provide it.

What's the actual payoff?

Visa and Mastercard discount your processing rate when you pass Level 3 data. Existing costs of 3-4% of your sales can drop to 2-3%, about a 25% reduction in your processing bill each month.

Do I need my customer to use the data?

No. Even if your customer never looks at it, you still lower your costs by passing it. Your customer can see the detail through their card-issuing bank whenever they want, easily.

Will I have to change banks or software?

No. We switch your processor seamlessly while you keep the same bank, accounting, and procedures. The virtual terminal runs from any computer or workstation in your office and integrates with major accounting platforms.

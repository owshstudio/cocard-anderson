Common questions

Frequently asked questions

What does CoCard Anderson do?

CoCard Anderson is a full-line merchant services and POS provider with offices in Syracuse, NY and Bennington, VT. We help businesses lower their card processing fees, set up modern POS systems, and get support from a local team that actually answers the phone. We have been serving the Northeast since 2002.

What is dual pricing?

Dual pricing lets you show one price for cash and another for card, the same way gas stations have for decades. It lets you recover up to 100% of your card processing fees, in a way that is compliant with Visa and Mastercard rules. You can see how it works and calculate your savings here.

What POS systems and terminals do you offer?

We offer EdgeServ, Genius, Bodega AI, and Clover POS systems, Dejavoo card terminals, and virtual terminals and gateways for online and over-the-phone payments. We match you to the right setup for how your business actually operates.

What areas do you serve?

We have local offices in Syracuse, NY and Bennington, VT, and we serve businesses across New York, Vermont, and Western Massachusetts.

Do you provide local support?

Yes. We are a local company, and a real person answers the phone. We handle setup, training, and ongoing support both in person and by phone, so you are never stuck in a call queue.

How do I get started?

Request a free, no-obligation quote and savings analysis. We review your current processing statement, show you what you would save, and handle the switch, keeping your existing bank and day-to-day procedures in place in most cases.

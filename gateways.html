How it works

The online layer, on top of your processing

A gateway is the secure checkout and virtual terminal that connects your website or back office to the card networks. We set it up on top of your CoCard merchant account, so you get a clean online experience and we keep handling the processing, the support, and your Cash Discount (dual pricing) savings.

  • Secure online checkout for your website
  • Virtual terminal to key in phone and mail orders
  • Payment links and recurring billing, depending on the gateway
  • Connected to your CoCard account, with local support

These are just some of the options.

Contact our office and we'll walk through additional gateways or find the best fit for how you sell, and pair it with Cash Discount (dual pricing). A real person always answers.

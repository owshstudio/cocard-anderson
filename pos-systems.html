Revonu

Revonu is CoCard's own reliable, robust POS. With restaurant, retail, and salon options, almost every merchant can benefit. Our all-in-one unit, tablet, EMV/MSR reader, and printer in a single terminal replacement, is our answer to Clover and Square, but on more robust cloud-based software.

  • All-in-one hardware: tablet + EMV/MSR reader + printer
  • Reliable cloud software with anytime, anywhere reporting
  • Loyalty and gift card integration built in
  • PCI-compliant processing, one provider for everything
  • Flexible, user-friendly cost of use merchants rave about

Coming soon: Revonu 2.0, cloud-based, iOS, full-service, flexible and agile.

Built for restaurants

MobileBytes

A cloud POS for restaurants that leverages the power and simplicity of Apple's iPad with cloud server technology. CoCard Anderson is the authorized MobileBytes distributor for upstate New York, Western Massachusetts, and Vermont, with flexible pricing that scales to your business.

  • Conversational ordering, tableside payments with signature capture
  • Native app keeps working through internet outages
  • Online ordering plus a guest engagement app for iOS & Android
  • Driver dispatch with turn-by-turn delivery mapping
  • Host & waitlist management with text messaging
  • Built-in time & attendance with photo verification
  • Electronic kitchen display and intuitive check splitting

  • No per-employee charge
  • Right mix of devices and apps tailored to how you operate

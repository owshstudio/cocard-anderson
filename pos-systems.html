iPad POS

EdgeServ

Our iPad-based system is both full-featured and affordable. It's intuitive, easy to use, and makes everything more efficient, so you run a tighter ship, increase guest counts, and keep more of your hard-earned cash.

  • Mobile solution lets servers take orders and payments tableside
  • Total EMV integration across every part of your business
  • Simple to use, keeping training time to a minimum
  • Build and run your own gift card and loyalty programs
  • Create and access reports from anywhere on your phone
  • Kitchen Display keeps orders moving and everyone in sync
  • Local customer service you can actually count on
Built for restaurants

MobileBytes

A cloud POS for restaurants that leverages the power and simplicity of Apple's iPad with cloud server technology. CoCard Anderson is the authorized MobileBytes distributor for upstate New York, Western Massachusetts, and Vermont, with flexible pricing that scales to your business.

  • Conversational ordering, tableside payments with signature capture
  • Native app keeps working through internet outages
  • Online ordering plus a guest engagement app for iOS & Android
  • Driver dispatch with turn-by-turn delivery mapping
  • Host & waitlist management with text messaging
  • Built-in time & attendance with photo verification
  • Electronic kitchen display and intuitive check splitting
AI-powered retail

Bodega AI

An AI-powered POS built for independent retail, convenience stores, bodegas, smoke shops, liquor stores, and delis, with scan-and-sell simplicity and the smarts to protect your margins.

  • Scan-and-sell checkout with automatic price scanning
  • AI profit optimization and zip-code-based pricing suggestions
  • Built-in dual pricing to protect your margins
  • License ID scanning and surcharge-law compliance
  • Tobacco scan-data rebate programs (Altria & RJRT)
  • Manage your store remotely, 24/7, in multiple languages
Smart terminals + cloud

iPOS

iPOS pairs Dejavoo's smart terminals with the DejaPay Pro cloud POS, so you can take payments in store and online from one simple, secure system, set up fast with minimal downtime.

  • EMV-certified payments with contactless, tap, and digital wallets
  • Flexible hardware: Dejavoo smart terminals on Android or iOS
  • Omnichannel support for in-store and online orders
  • Real-time analytics for smarter day-to-day decisions
  • Inventory and employee productivity tracking
  • Advanced security with encryption and tokenization
Popular & flexible

Clover

A point-of-sale system designed to make things easy. Take orders, accept payments, organize inventory, and manage your team, all at your fingertips. The basics are built in so you can set up right away, and because we don't charge per employee, the whole team can use it.

  • Syncs with the cloud, access sales, refunds, and reports anywhere
  • Scales with your business, add devices, apps, and accessories any time
  • No per-employee charge
  • Right mix of devices and apps tailored to how you operate

Why it matters now

Credit cards used to be 30% of your sales. Now they're nearly all of it.

That wasn't a big deal when card use was a third of your transactions. But today, depending on your business, it's 70-99%, and more often than not those are expensive rewards cards. Our typical customer is paying tens of thousands of dollars a year in credit card fees.

So ask yourself the question we ask every business owner: why should you pay for your customers' mileage rewards?

CoCard Anderson provides highly vetted tools that let you offer a discount for cash, or a surcharge for credit, exactly the way gas stations have for decades. Together, that approach is known as dual pricing. Either way, you recover your cost of acceptance and we explain the right way to do it, fully within the card issuers' rules.

Common questions

Cash Discount FAQ

Is this actually legal?

Yes, 100%. Offering a discount for cash is explicitly permitted, and surcharging for credit is allowed within Visa and Mastercard rules and recent high-court judgments. The Durbin Amendment to the Dodd-Frank Financial Reform Act opened the door, and these programs are quickly becoming mainstream. We make sure yours is set up the right way and stays fully compliant.

What does it cost me to start?

Nothing to set up. There's zero setup or activation fee, and the required Visa/Mastercard disclosure signage is free. You also get 24/7 telephone technical support included.

Will my customers be upset?

It's the same model they already see every day at the gas pump. With clear signage and a small, transparent difference between cash and card pricing, the vast majority of customers don't blink, and many simply choose to pay with cash or debit.

How much will I really save?

Our fixed-fee programs reduce transaction costs by over 95%, and most merchants recover effectively 100% of their processing fees. The exact number depends on your volume and card mix, which is why we run a free, no-obligation analysis on your real statement before you commit to anything.

Do I have to switch banks or POS systems?

No. In most cases we keep your existing bank, accounting, and procedures in place. We handle the setup, the signage, and the training, and we're local, so when you need us, a real person answers.

The problem we solve

The convenience parents want, without the cost you can't afford

Most childcare centers don't offer card payments because the processing cost cuts straight into already-thin margins. But whether it's for convenience, rewards, or simple necessity, most parents want the option to pay by card.

With CoCard Anderson, you can accept tuition payments by check and credit card online with no fees to your business. Parents pay the processing fees, and your facility gets the full amount due. You choose whether to pass on some or all of the fees.

No cost to you
